Understanding your rights and responsibilities when using CubeKit components.

CubeKit is a commercial product licensed per developer at $59/year. You can use CubeKit in unlimited commercial projects, but each developer needs their own license.

✓ Commercial Use

Use in commercial projects and SaaS applications.

✓ Unlimited Projects

Create unlimited applications with one license.

What You Cannot Do

✗ Redistribution

Cannot redistribute or resell components.

✗ Sharing Licenses

Each developer must have their own license.
